dagat

(noun, meaning sea or ocean)

dagat is a casual dining restaurant ready to satisfy your seafood cravings. Our mission is to offer our customers the freshest and most delicious seafood dishes while providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere that reflects the vibrant culture of the Philippines. In addition to seafood, we also offer a range of other traditional Filipino dishes, such as our succulent lechon kawali (crispy pork belly) and our famous chicken adobo.

We look forward to serving you the best seafood and traditional Filipino dishes that will make you feel like you've been transported to the islands of the Philippines.
